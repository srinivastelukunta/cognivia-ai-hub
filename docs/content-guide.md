## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics
- **Professional yet approachable:** Expert without being academic
- **Confident and authoritative:** Based on real results and research
- **Action-oriented:** Focus on implementation, not theory
- **Honest and transparent:** Acknowledge challenges alongside successes

### Writing Style
- Short paragraphs and scannable content
- Concrete examples over abstract concepts
- Active voice and strong verbs
- Avoid hype and buzzwords
- Lead with outcomes and value

### Example Transformations

❌ **Avoid:** "Leverage cutting-edge AI technologies to revolutionize your digital transformation journey"

✅ **Use:** "Deploy AI systems that deliver 10-25% EBITDA gains within 18 months"

❌ **Avoid:** "Thought leadership from industry luminaries"

✅ **Use:** "Frameworks from CDAIOs who've scaled AI across Fortune 500 enterprises"
